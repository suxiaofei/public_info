### 1. No Data Collection and Use
The Application is designed as a fully offline, free, and ad-free game. 
* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, IP address, or device identifiers).

### 2. Third-Party Services
The Application does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. No third party has access to your device or data through this Application.

### 3. Children's Privacy
Because the Application does not collect any personal information, it is fully safe for children and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
